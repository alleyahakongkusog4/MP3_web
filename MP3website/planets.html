Interesting Facts About Planets

  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system.
  • Venus has the longest day of any planet.
  • Mars is home to the tallest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.
  • Saturn's rings are made mostly of ice particles.
  • Uranus rotates on its side, making it unique among the planets.
  • Neptune was the first planet discovered through mathematical calculations.
